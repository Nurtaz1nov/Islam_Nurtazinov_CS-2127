Who are they?

he Avengers are a fictional team of superheroes and the protagonists of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) media franchise, based on the Marvel Comics team of the same name created by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby in 1963.

Team members

Iron Man,Thor,Hulk,Captain America,Black Widow,Doctor Strange,Spider Man,Black Panther,Wanda Maximoff,Hawkeye,Ant-man.

Who assembled the Avengers?

The Avengers are a team of powerful superheroes assembled by Nick Fury and S.H.I.E.L.D. with the help of the Avengers Initiative, with the aim of fighting battles in which they would not stand alone.
